# 🌿 UMUHINZI AI

**UMUHINZI AI** is a world-class agricultural assistant designed for farmers in Rwanda and beyond. It combines cutting-edge AI technology with real-time weather, market data, and community interaction to empower modern farming.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Expert AI Chatbot:** Get scientifically accurate advice on crop management, pests, and local conditions.
*   **Real-Time Weather:** Hyper-local weather data with specific agricultural recommendations.
*   **2026 Market Insights:** Live-simulated Rwandan market prices for better financial planning.
*   **Crop Diagnosis:** Upload a photo of your crop to identify diseases and get prevention tips.
*   **Community Forums:** Share your experiences and learn from other farmers in a social environment.
*   **Multi-Language Support:** Fully translated into Kinyarwanda, Swahili, French, English, and more.
*   **PWA (Downloadable):** Install the app directly on your phone or computer for a native experience.

## 📂 Development & Contribution

This project is built using modern web technologies.
